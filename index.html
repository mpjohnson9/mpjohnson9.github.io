About Chi Phi

The Beta Chapter of Chi Phi holds itself to a very high standard. We hope that through the eternal bonds created when living in this house, we can become better men not just during our four years, but for the rest of our lives. Our strong-willed spirit defines us and proudly resonates with the lessons that brothers of yesteryear have engrained into our brotherhood. The gentlemen of the Beta Chapter will never cease to uphold high standards for academics, behavior, and morals. Whether in the classroom or on the athletic field, in the chapter house or out in the community, in the term or on holiday, our members never forget what it is to be a Chi Phi. We strive to maintain values that others in society have dared to compromise and hold those values with us as new members and onward. We shed every negative stereotype applied to fraternities and demonstrate that our organization is one that is to be revered and respected for the values we maintain.
